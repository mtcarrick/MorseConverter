MorseConverter

Converts letters and numbers to Morse Code and vice-versa. WIP

To run:

$ ./morse.py

Enter your string: <input in English>

<output in morse code>

morse.py will only work when given the letters a-z, digits 1-9, and the special characters ?, ,, and .. All other characters will result in an error.
